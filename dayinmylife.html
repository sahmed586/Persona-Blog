A Day in My Life

What we do on a day to day basis is something that is often times overlooked, when in reality, each day is a building block of how we picture our overall "life". That being said, it is extremely important to spend each day wisely and allocate our time towards things that matter to us.

The following is a rough skeleton of how I aim to go through a typical weekday.

  • Wake up before sunrise for prayer.
  • Afterwards, I usually go back to sleep, depending on work load and energy I may stay up.
  • Wake up again in a few hours to complete my rest.
  • Eat a healthy breakfast while watching lectures and taking notes.
  • Go to the gym (every other day). The days I don't go I tend to complete any chores I have.
  • Typically second prayer of the day.
  • Lunch time!
  • Post Lunch is when I do some last minute preparation for quizzes and exams.
  • Third prayer of the day.
  • Complete any quizzes exams. This is also when I finally get to spend time with my family once everyone is at home together.
  • Fourth prayer of the day.
  • Dinner together with the family.
  • Final prayer of the day.
  • Self care time and unwinding for the night!

However, on the weekends, my schedule looks a little different because it includes my work schedule. Currently I am working at a dress boutique at a local mall. The following is my schedule on a work day, which is normally on the weekends.

  • Aim to wake up for sunrise time prayer.
  • Go back to sleep to make sure I am well rested for the day.
  • Wake up again at 10:30 am and start preparing myself for the day.
  • At about 11 am I try to eat a full balanced breakfast to carry me through the rest of the day.
  • I then get myself dressed in whatever I want to wear and do my makeup.
  • After this I pack myself a small snack for later in the day and water.
  • Head out of my house at approvimately 11:30am so I can arrive to my shift 10 minutes early.
  • My shift starts at noon, so I work from then on helping customers find the dresses they are looking for as well as maintain a clean and safe environment.
  • My shift ends approximately at 7, so I then head home and typically clean up and shower.
  • My plan for the rest of the day is either to spend time with family, or take care of school work dpending on my workload that week.

Often times things get in the way and we go through our ups and downs that don't always allow us to complete all that we would like to do on any given day. Just like today June 1st, 2021, I was not able to follow this schedule exactlty. Lack of motivation is something that I struggle with in waves every now and then and it makes it very difficult to complete daily tasks. But as stated by Winston Churchill:

A pessimist sees diffculty in every opportunity. The optimist sees the opportunity in every difficulty.

It is so easy to get caught up in the struggle and view things from a negative perspective especially with how much our society has changed within this past year due to the pandemic, as stated in Forbes Magazine Article The Pandemic Has Created A New Kind Of Burnout Which Makes Well-Being More Critical Than Ever by Naz Beheshti.

However, in the end it is so important to remind yourself that there is a beauty behind the struggle. It is a lot easier to say than to believe it, especially during difficult times but glimpses of optimism - hopefulness and condifence about the future, can go a long way.
